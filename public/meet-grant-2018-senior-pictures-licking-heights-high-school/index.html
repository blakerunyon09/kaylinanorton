Meet Grant: 2018 Senior Pictures | Licking Heights High School

portraits / seniors

Meet Grant. He's a senior at Licking Heights High School in Pataskala, Ohio. He plays football, runs track, and spends his spare time playing video games (current favorite: Destiny 2). He'll graduate in the spring and plans on attending Ohio University. I loved spending Sunday morning with him at Licking Heights, exploring the football stadium and the surrounding woods. Grant is easy going and laid back, and because I'm sure this blog post is already going to embarrass him, I'm just going to say it - super cute. Am I the only one that gets a young Matt Damon vibe?! Haha, sorry, Grant! Thanks so much to Grant, his mom, and step-dad for spending Sunday morning with me! I can't wait to see what the next few years brings to this amazing family as Grant transitions from high school to college! Best of luck! Keep smiling! Kaylina
