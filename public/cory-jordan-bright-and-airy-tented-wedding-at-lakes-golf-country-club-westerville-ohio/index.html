Cory + Jordan: Bright and Airy Tented Wedding at Lakes Golf & Country Club | Westerville, Ohio

weddings

I walked into the women's locker room at the Lakes Golf & Country Club in Westerville last Friday afternoon to a flurry of activity. Jordan and her 12 bridesmaids were putting the finishing touches on their hair and make-up while, on the other side of the building, Cory and his groomsmen casually sat outside on the huge, wrap-around porch of the country club enjoying the warm breeze and playing cards. Their wedding day was perfect - the grey clouds that loomed overhead at the beginning of the day gave way to golden sunshine and the nervous energy that ruled the morning became pure joy as Cory and Jordan said "I do" in front of their friends and family in a sun-soaked white tent on the grounds of the country club. Jordan is a graphic designer here in Columbus and she did an amazing job designing the invitation suite, programs, and menus for the wedding. Her classic shoes, lush bouquet, simple jewelry, and breathtaking dress were equally gorgeous and the perfect accents to Jordan's big day. Jordan and Cory's ceremony was beautiful. Light streamed into the tent from all sides while the couple exchanged rings and their own written vows. After the ceremony, we headed out to a gorgeous weeping willow on the country club's property to celebrate and finish bridal party pictures. After we sent the bridal party to grab some drinks, Cory and Jordan spent twenty minutes posing, laughing, and walking the property - looking AMAZING every step of the way.  Seriously, these two are the cutest!
