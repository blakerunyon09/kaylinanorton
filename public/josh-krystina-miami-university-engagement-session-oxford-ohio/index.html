Josh + Krystina: Miami University Engagement Session - Oxford, Ohio

engagements

Josh and Krystina first met during high school when Josh was a senior and Krystina was a junior. They struck up a friendship during Josh's freshmen year at Miami University and by the time Krystina started attending the university the next fall, the two were smitten. Walking around together on one of Krystina's first night on campus, Josh snuck a kiss under Upham Arch. As legend has it, couples who share a kiss under Upham Arch will be together forever and there's even a name for two Miami student marrying each other: a Miami Merger. With this adorable backstory and the memories that Josh and Krystina have of their time together at Miami, it was the obvious choice for location for their engagement session this past weekend. I had Josh and Krystina lead me around campus and listened to their stories about each building we passed. These two are so much to be around with such easy going personalities. I cannot wait for their merger to be official in May! Josh and Krystina - thank you so much for taking me on a tour of Miami! It's such a gorgeous campus and I can understand how you two fell in love with it! I am so excited for your wedding in May and I cannot wait to meet the puppies! Keep smiling! Kaylina
