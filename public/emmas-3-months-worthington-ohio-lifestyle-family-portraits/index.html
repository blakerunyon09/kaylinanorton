Emma's 3 Months: Worthington, Ohio Lifestyle Family Portraits

baby / family / portraits

Bundled up in sweaters and boots, Little Miss Emma and her family were ready to take the most adorable fall family pictures. There was only one problem - despite it being the end of October in Ohio, the sun was shining and it was 75 degrees out last Saturday! So they pretended like they weren't all burning up in their fashionable fall attire and we had a great time exploring the streets of Old Worthington! Emma was wide awake and alert for her in-home newborn shoot in July and I have to say she still does a great job of looking at the camera! Both she and her big sister, Addison, are well trained since their mommy happens to be a photographer herself! This "blooper" might be my favorite picture from the whole day! Emma's face! And Addison following directions and smiling for the camera no matter what! Love it! Keep smiling! Kaylina
