Meet the K Family: Heritage Park Family Portraits

family / portraits

I've had a mini-vacation from weddings the last couple of weekends and have filled my extra time with the best thing: adorable little ones! This past Saturday I met the K family at Heritage Park in Westerville for a quick family session to celebrate Jack's first birthday! Jack and his older brother, Charlie, kept me on my toes and never left a dull moment. My favorite thing about family pictures are the bloopers between the perfect smiling ones, they're fun and give a little glimpse into reality to look back on for years to come! When it was time for Jack's close-up, he just wasn't having it. But who can blame him? Being one is hard. And we just had to get some quick pics of mom and dad before the session was over! Keep smiling! Kaylina
