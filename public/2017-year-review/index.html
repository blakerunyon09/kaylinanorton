2017: A Year in Review

personal

You guys, where did the year go? I seriously cannot believe it is already time to pack up the Christmas decorations and pull out the new calendars. 2017 had its ups and downs, but one thing is for sure: it flew by! I've already caught myself accidentally dating things 2018 so I think I'm ready to ring in the new year, but before we do, I want to take one final look back on all of the amazing people I was blessed to meet and work with through the last 12 months. Every year I pull one image from my shoots throughout the year and it is always so hard. This year though, I cheated a little bit and used Instagram's insights to show me which of the photos from each shoot were the favorites of my followers, so it made my job just a touch easier ;) Shameless plug time: if you're not already following me on Instagram, you should do that now! You can find me sharing sneak peeks from my sessions (and photos of my cats) at @kaylinanorton! A million, bajillion thank you's to all of the amazing people who let me take their pictures this year. I was able to be a part of engagements, weddings, new babies, and wonderful families. It's super cheesy, but I am always so thankful that people entrust me with their precious memories. I hope everyone had an amazing 2017 and that 2018 has more hugs, kisses, and (happy) tears planned for you! Keep smiling! Kaylina
