Meet Emma: Lifestyle Newborn Photos - New Albany, Ohio

baby / lifestyle / portraits

Meet Emma. She's tiny, adorable, and the most well behaved 12 day old baby I've ever met. Emma was wide awake for her entire newborn photo shoot this morning, just studying everything her mommy, daddy, and big sister were doing. I absolutely love doing these lifestyle newborn shoots - capturing some of the early moments of a family bonding and getting to know the newest addition to the family is so much fun. And the excitement Emma's big sister, Addison had over her new baby sister was heartwarming! Welcome to the world, Emma! You are so loved! Keep smiling!
