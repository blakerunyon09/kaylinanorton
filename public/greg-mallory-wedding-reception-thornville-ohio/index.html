Greg + Mallory: Wedding Reception Thornville, Ohio

weddings

Last Saturday I had the pleasure of shooting a wedding reception for Greg and Mallory, who got married earlier this year in Australia. Greg and Mallory are a gorgeous couple who put together an amazing evening for their friends and family in Ohio to celebrate their love. It was a little cloudy at Buckeye Lake Winery but the rain held off and everyone was able to mingle and enjoy themselves outside on the patio before heading inside for dinner.







Mallory did all of the beautiful centerpieces and flowers herself and Made From Scratch provided the delicious food. Greg and Mallory had an amazing photographer, Verity of Ginger and Mint Photography, at their wedding in Australia and had photos of the big day on display all over the winery on Saturday.




I loved snapping pictures of Greg and Mallory interacting with their guests. They always had smiles on their faces and were truly enjoying the company of their friends and family.









Strung Up Bluegrass did a great job providing the entertainment at dinner, which everyone loved. 
Well, almost everyone...


Greg and Mallory- thank you so much for having me at your reception! It was such a pleasure to meet you and capture your day!

Keep smiling!
Kaylina
