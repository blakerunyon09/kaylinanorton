Adam + Elisa: Licking County Courthouse Wedding

weddings

There's a certain charm to a small town courthouse. When the sun is shining and a light breeze is blowing through the recently changed autumn leaves, you can't ask for a better back drop for an intimate courthouse wedding. Adam and Elisa said "I do" in front of a judge (and my camera) last Monday and had a beautiful afternoon to explore the surrounding town square of downtown Newark, Ohio for a few pictures. Adam was dapper in Express, but Elisa stole the show in head to toe BHLDN. Seriously, she was GORGEOUS and every single person we passed complimented her on her dress, shoes, and jewelry! Adam and Elisa - thank you so much for letting me be a part of such a special day in your lives! I absolutely loved every detail from your day and exploring Newark with you was so much fun! Congratulations!! Keep smiling!
