Kamburowski Family Photos: Sharon Woods Metro Park, Columbus, Ohio

family / portraits

We have officially turned the corner into cold weather which means that outdoor portrait sessions are starting to slow down, but a little cold weather doesn't scare the Kamburowski family! We met up last Saturday afternoon for a quick outdoor family picture shoot at Sharon Woods Metro Park in Westerville, Ohio. Jack and Charlie are two little sweethearts and I had so much fun with them and their amazing mommy and daddy as we snapped a few pictures for the family's Christmas card! About halfway through the session we put the coats and winter hats on and snuggled up in front a giant Christmas tree for the last part of the shoot! These two are just too cute!!   Keep smiling! Kaylina
