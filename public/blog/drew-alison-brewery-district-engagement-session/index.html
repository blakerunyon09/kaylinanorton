Drew + Alison: Brewery District Engagement Session

engagements

  It might be her larger than life hair or her one million watt smile, but whatever it is, Alison is the type of girl who just draws people to her. She'll be the first to admit, people remember meeting her. Drew is one of those people who found themselves drawn to Alison and soon fell in love. He decided to propose at Camp Fitch, a place that's played a huge part in her life and many fond memories. Although they had talked about marriage before, the timing of his proposal completely surprised her. She had no idea it was coming, or of the small hiccup in the plan when Drew initially forgot to bring the ring. But when it was all said and done, she said yes, of course, and now they're planning a May wedding with all of their friends and family at Via Vecchia Winery in the Brewery District in Columbus, Ohio. We started our shoot at Via Vecchia on Sunday, taking advantage of the gorgeous brick alley where the venue holds outdoor wedding ceremonies and a nearby loading dock that had plenty of photogenic wood pallets.
I absolutely adore Drew and Alison- they have a kind of chemistry that required no coaching. If I said "flirt" Drew didn't need to be told twice. And of course, Alison's smile and hair always seemed to steal the show.
Alison's laugh and the ease that Drew had in bringing it out of her - perfection.
These two are so much fun. Somewhere before our last outfit change, we fell on the topic of The Amazing Race and Alison's competitive side. Although we all agreed that Drew and Alison are far too functional of a couple to be picked to be on the show, I still wouldn't want to challenge these two to a race. I could definitely see them being a force to be reckoned with. We ended the night in a beautiful grassy area a few blocks South of Via Vecchia on Front Street. The lighting was gorgeous and I was so happy we could end the shoot in a soft, intimate setting.
 
 
Drew and Alison- Thank you so much for spending the evening with me! I had such a great time getting to know you two and cannot wait for the wedding in May! I know you two are the perfect fit for each other and will think of a million ways to keep each other on your toes and laughing for years and years to come. Keep smiling! Kaylina Update: Drew and Alison are married! Check out their Brewery District Wedding at Via Vecchia!
