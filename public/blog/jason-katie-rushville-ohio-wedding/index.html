Jason + Katie: Rushville, Ohio Wedding

weddings



If I had to describe Jason and Katie in one word it would be "fun". Yes, I realize that "fun" doesn't exactly impress anyone with my vocabulary and it would be more descriptive to launch into talking about how their interactions with each other brings a smile to anyone's face who's lucky enough to be around them, how Jason adores teasing Katie, and how Katie's honesty is refreshing and hilarious, but if given just one word- I would choose "fun". Because that's what they are, that is what their wedding day was, and that's what their marriage will be.

Katie started out her day getting ready at Lancaster's Hampton Inn. While I took detail photo of shoes and jewelry in the lobby, Katie and her support team of friends and family chatted casually while they got their hair done. I asked Katie if she was nervous and she told me it didn't feel real yet.








We stopped by the nearby mall to get Katie's make-up done at Ulta where it still didn't feel real yet and moved on to her friend's home in Rushville where the wedding was being held. It was an absolutely gorgeous day and the tent, decorations, and food only added to the beauty of the day.
 






 







The moment when it suddenly felt real...


Jason was slightly more nervous that day and spent his time before the ceremony outside with his daughter.




The ceremony itself was beautiful and I absolutely loved the way Jason was looking at Katie the entire way through. He adores her, and it's wonderful to see.













After the ceremony Katie, Jason, and I found the only spot of shade on the property and started snapping some pictures. I loved the way they interacted, perfectly at home with each other.




It's always nice to get real laughs, even if they're laughing at me.

 


Katie- you are absolutely stunning. Seriously.
 

 

After the portraits we headed back to the tent for the reception where the fun could really begin.





After the cake cutting, Katie and Jason challenged their friends to step up and perform "Ice, Ice Baby" and they did not disappoint...



Sometimes I can't help but love myself a country wedding...







When the sun went down Jason slipped into something a little more comfortable...

Jason and Katie, you guys are amazing, fun, and spectacular. It was such an honor to be a part of your love story. Thank you so much for letting Blake and I tag along throughout the day! I hope you're having a wonderful time on your honeymoon!
