Ryan + Ashley: Schiller Park Engagement Session

engagements

Neither of them were really looking for a relationship. His friend liked her friend so they each found themselves getting dragged along to the bar that night for moral support. After Ryan introduced himself to the girls, he noticed Ashley sitting a little ways away from the group. She had recently lost her voice and didn't want to make matters worse by shouting above the din. But Ryan's persistence set history in motion. They talked a little, got to know each other, and at the end of the night when he asked for her number... she gave him a fake one! Not to spoil the ending, but these two beautiful and hilarious people are getting married next July. We shot Ryan and Ashley's engagement pictures in Schiller Park last weekend and getting to hear them tell the story of how they met and fell in love was the highlight of my night. Ashley is the sweetest girl you could imagine and mixing her side of the story with Ryan's mischievous grin as he interjected his version of the truth about that night kept me laughing all night. Ryan and Ashley are an adorable couple who deserve to be together. They compliment each other perfectly and their playful banter helped bring these photos to life. Ryan and Ashley - thank you so much for spending the evening with me! I had so much fun getting to know you two and the love story you have started together! I cannot wait to be there on your wedding day next year as you start the next chapter! P.S. You guys are seriously TOO CUTE! Keep smiling! Kaylina
