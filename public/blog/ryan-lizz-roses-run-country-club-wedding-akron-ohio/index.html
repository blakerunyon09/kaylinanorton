Ryan + Lizz: Roses Run Country Club Wedding - Akron, Ohio

sunset session / weddings

74 degrees without a cloud in the sky, Lizz and Ryan had the perfect day to say "I do" last Saturday. The morning started out calm and casual in Stow where the guys and girls got ready in separate hotel rooms. Starting out the day with details, I immediately fell in love with Lizz's Jimmy Choo heels. Just the right amount of glitz and glam for a wedding day! Lizz looked like a movie star in her wedding dress - tall, blonde, and sun-kissed! After everyone had arrived at St. Vincent De Paul Church in Akron, it was finally the moment everyone had been waiting for - let's get these two married! After a gorgeous church ceremony, guests made their way over to Roses Run Country Club in Stow, Ohio for the party. Ryan, Lizz, and the bridal party took over the golf course with their golf carts for some gorgeous pictures on the course. I was obsessed with Lizz's bridesmaids dresses, bouquets, and jewelry - the bracelets and earrings were gifts to the girls from Lizz and complimented the Adrianna Papell dresses perfectly. And how amazing did Ryan look as a dapper groom?! After pictures it was officially party time! The reception hall at Roses Run Country Club was beautifully decorated and was an amazing backdrop for the night's festivities. After toasts, the cake cutting, and first dances, the Marino Brothers kept the dance floor full all night with their own killer dance moves. At the end of the night, Ryan and Lizz treated their guests to gyros from Gyro George, a local food truck with delicious gyros and onion rings. Ryan and Lizz, thank you so much for allowing us to be a part of your big day! It was so much fun to spend the day with you, your family, and giant bridal party! I hope you two have an amazing time on your honeymoon and I can't wait to see what the future has in store for you! Keep smiling!
