Meet Josh: 2017 Central Ohio High School Senior Portraits

portraits / seniors

Hello world, meet Josh. Josh is a 2017 senior at Kenston High School in Chagrin Falls, Ohio. He's a lacrosse and football stud who is looking at making Ohio University his home next fall. And if this description is starting to sound like an online dating profile it might just be because Josh is also my cousin and I happen to think he's amazing and enjoy embarrassing him at the same time ;) Josh was a reluctant participant in his own senior pictures, but as all good sons do, chose to humor his mom for an hour last Sunday. We struck a deal right off the bat - 3 poses in 3 outfits and he would be done. And although his mom snuck in a fourth outfit at the end, I like to think the process was as painless as possible. We were in and out in under an hour and got some pictures that both he and his mom can be happy with. Mission accomplished. Keep smiling! Kaylina
