Kamburowski Family: Family Portraits Inniswoods | Westerville, Ohio

family / portraits

Starting my Sunday mornings with family pictures has become one of my favorite weekly traditions. This Sunday was filled with the charming Kamburowski boys, Charlie and Jack. I've been photographing this family for a year now and love to see how these cute guys have grown! Charlie is still as rambunctious as ever, but is learning to reign in that little-kid-CHEESEEEEEE smile. Jack, who is normally full of giggles, was a little more solemn on Sunday, but definitely more mobile than the last time I had the pleasure of shooting this amazing family. Our session on Sunday was at Inniswoods Metro Garden in Westerville. It's a big park, but we had Charlie lead the way and pick out the locations he wanted to take pictures in along the way. I have to say, he did a great job location scouting for us! Keep smiling! Kaylina
