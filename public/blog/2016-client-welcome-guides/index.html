2016 Client Welcome Guides

for clients / wedding planning

This past weekend I packaged up and shipped out my 2016 Client Welcome Guides. This is an idea and a project I've been working on for almost 10 months now (designing and creating your own magazine by hand takes a little longer than I thought it would) and I'm so excited to have these goodies out the door and on their way to all of my 2016 brides and grooms! Here's what's in the boxes:  1) They include my "Wedding Handbook", a magazine that walks clients through all the aspects of wedding photography and what they can do to help prepare for the best wedding photography experience. Everything from what to wear to your engagement session to when you should make your big exit on the wedding day is covered. 2) An adorable notebook (and pencil) with lined pages for brides and grooms to keep track of their to-do lists, ideas, and dreams while they finish their wedding planning journey. 3) A thank you note, because I can never thank my clients enough for entrusting me with their big day! 4) Chocolates - no explanation necessary.
