Goodman Family: Lifestyle Family Portraits | New Albany, Ohio

family / kids / lifestyle / portraits

No one says you can't have family pictures done when it's 20 degrees out. Actually, winter sessions are some of my favorites - I love doing in-home lifestyle family portraits! A few posed smiles are involved, but mostly contagious giggles, un-ending silliness, and beautiful, candid smiles. I went over to the Goodman's house last weekend to meet Claire, their almost 2-year-old bundle of joy. She was so much fun to chase around as she bounced from one room to another. We started out in the living room, visited her playroom for some reading, sliding, and dancing, before heading to mommy & daddy's room for some jumping on the bed! Keep smiling! Kaylina
