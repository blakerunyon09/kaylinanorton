KJ + Jamie: Zion Church Wedding | Pickerington, Ohio

first look / sunset session / weddings

When I met with KJ and Jamie last October for their engagement session at Slate Run, I immediately knew their wedding was going to be a lot of fun. Both KJ and Jamie are easy going, wonderful, and so kind. Their love for each other, their family, and their friends made photographing their wedding last Saturday such a pleasure. Arriving at Zion Pickerington Church Saturday afternoon, I found Jamie and her bridesmaids in the nursery with a giant bag of skittles and even bigger smiles on their faces. The guys got ready on the other side of the church before we ultimately pulled KJ and Jamie together for a first look and bridal party portraits before the ceremony. After the ceremony, KJ and Jamie spent the evening visiting with family and friends. Right before sunset, we snuck outside for a few quick pictures before the rain started again. Great food and great music fueled the rest of the night which was capped off by a wonderful sparkler send-off. Congrats KJ and Jamie! Blake and I had such a great time documenting your day! I cannot wait to see what the future has in store for you two! Keep smiling! Kaylina So much love for these amazing vendors who made their day perfect!
