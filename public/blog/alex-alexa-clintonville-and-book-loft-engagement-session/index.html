Alex + Alexa: Clintonville and Book Loft Engagement Session

engagements

Alex and Alexa fit together perfectly. The way they melt into each other when they're next to each other, the way her smile contagiously spreads to his face when he looks at her. There is one thing that is absolutely certain: Alex and Alexa belong together. They met online and spent their first few dates exploring Columbus together. From Bodega to Gallery Hop, they got to know each other, but it wasn't until they visited The Book Loft in German Village that things really clicked. Their love of books and adventure brought them together so it was only fitting we incorporated these aspects into their engagement pictures! We met up at Glen Echo Park in Clintonville Sunday evening for some beautiful fall engagement pictures before heading to the Book Loft in German Village to finish up the session. Alex and Alexa did an amazing job and were such a pleasure to work with! Alex and Alexa - thank you so much for hanging out with me on Sunday! I had such a great time exploring Glen Echo Park with you and having you as my guides through all 32 twisting rooms of The Book Loft! I am so excited for your wedding in July! Keep smiling! Kaylina
