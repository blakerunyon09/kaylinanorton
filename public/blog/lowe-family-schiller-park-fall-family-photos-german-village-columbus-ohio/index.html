Lowe Family: Schiller Park Fall Family Photos | German Village, Columbus, Ohio

family / portraits

Photographing the Lowe family for their family portraits this past fall in German Village was so much fun. They're a picture perfect family that had me feeling like I was in a J Crew catalogue all morning. Seriously, I couldn't get enough of their matching outfits, beautiful smiles, and the gorgeous colors that Schiller Park had on display for us! Due to the cold in the air, we kept the session short, but that was still plenty of time to laugh, tickle, and have an epic leaf fight. These photos are fun, vibrant, and full of laughter - I hope these memories are on their walls for years to come!   Keep smiling! Kaylina
