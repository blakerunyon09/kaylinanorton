Ryan + Lizz: Ohio Stadium Engagement Session Columbus, Ohio

engagements

There are two things that Ryan and Lizz love most in this world: The Ohio State Buckeyes and their dogs. So naturally they wanted to incorporate both of those things into their engagement session last Sunday! We met in front of the Horse Shoe on OSU's campus Sunday evening for some quick pictures before the weather got too cold and although the dogs were a little bit of a hand full, I couldn't think of cuter models to pose with! Ryan and Lizz - thank you so much for braving the chilly weather with me! I loved getting to know your buckeye loving family and am so excited for the two of you! Congratulations and best of luck with the rest of your wedding planning! July will be here before you know it! Keep smiling! Kaylina
