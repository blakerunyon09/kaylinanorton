Meet Sam: 2018 Senior Portraits | Mount Vernon, Ohio

portraits / seniors

Meet Sam. He's a super smart senior who is actually spending his senior year taking classes at Mount Vernon Nazarene University. He'll graduate in the spring and but his brain to great use with his interest in programming, but for now, he's a senior. And like all senior boys, his mom forced him to endure a senior portrait session last weekend ;) Sam was a great sport and spent an hour with me exploring the Hoover Reservoir Park in New Albany. I had a great time getting to know him as we walked around the park, sticking to the shade as much as possible because Ohio is still unreasonably hot this time of year. Thanks so much to Sam and his parents for spending their Sunday evening with me. Sam, best of luck next year! Can't wait to see what the future holds! Keep smiling! Kaylina
