Tim + Brielle: Homestead Park Engagement Session - Hilliard, Ohio

engagements

When I was little I remember begging my mom for a little sister and although I never got my wish, I just had to wait a couple of years for my cousin Brielle to make an appearance at family events. She was amazing and adorable and I was obsessed with my new baby cousin. Brie has remained amazing and adorable throughout the last two decades, but she is no longer my baby cousin because she's getting married!!! Tim is an amazing man and the perfect match for Brie. He's funny and kind and we could not be more thrilled to welcome him to our crazy family. I am so excited that these two are engaged and will be tying the knot next June and I am honored to be there to capture it all on (digital) film! We met up for Tim and Brie's engagement pictures at Homestead Park in Hilliard, Ohio on Saturday afternoon. Homestead Park's white Ohio Bicentennial Barn and flower filled meadows is the perfect backdrop for a sweet engagement session. Dark clouds in the sky threatened rain one minute only to quickly disappear and leave a sweltering hot sun behind, but Tim and Brie were champs the entire time. Tim and Brie, thank you for being incredible people and an insanely cute couple. I had so much fun spending the afternoon with you and I cannot wait to officially welcome Tim to the family next June! Keep smiling! Kaylina
