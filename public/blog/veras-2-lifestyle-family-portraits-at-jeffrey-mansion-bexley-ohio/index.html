Vera's 2: Lifestyle Family Portraits at Jeffrey Mansion - Bexley, Ohio

baby / family / portraits

My oh my, Little Miss Vera is such a joy to be around! I first met her last fall for some fun in-home lifestyle portraits of her and her amazing mommy and daddy. This fall we met at Jeffrey Mansion in Bexley to get some family pictures taken. I couldn't get over how big (and opinionated) Vera's gotten in the last year! This little 2 year old was my boss for the morning! We spent an hour wandering around Jeffrey Mansion, letting Vera pick out where she wanted to go next and what she wanted to do. Vera had us walking on logs, jumping up and down, swinging on swings, and laughing along the way! Keep smiling! Kaylina
