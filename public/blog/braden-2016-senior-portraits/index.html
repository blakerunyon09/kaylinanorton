Braden: 2016 Liberty Union HS Senior Portraits

portraits / seniors

Meet Braden - he's a 2016 Senior at Liberty Union High School. He plays soccer, loves to hunt, and you'll rarely find him without his GoPro attached to him, recording his adventures skiing, tubing, and wakeboarding. As amazing as Braden is, there's one thing that makes him even cooler: he's my cousin! And although he doesn't have any definite plans for after high school yet, I'm sure whatever he ends up doing he'll be amazing, because it runs in the family =) I met up with Braden and his mom Sunday morning at Rising Park in Lancaster, Ohio to snap some senior photos. I am well aware that boys aren't exactly excited to get their senior pictures taken, so I promised him it would be quick and painless. And I at least delivered on the quick part. One hour later, we were done and, other than the fact we were all extremely sweaty from the climb up and down Mt. Pleasant,  no worse for the wear!
Braden was such a good sport and I happen to think that if he didn't hate pictures so much he could have a modeling career in front of him. He's got a great smile when he feels like showing it! Keep smiling! Kaylina
