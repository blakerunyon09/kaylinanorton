4th of July Summer Maternity Session | Inniswood Gardens, Westerville, Ohio

family / portraits

I love hanging out with this amazing family. I first met Annina and her parents last fall for a quick family session in downtown Worthington and was so excited when they reached out to do another session while they were in town for the 4th of July. There was one very exciting thing different this time though: Annina's mommy is pregnant! We shot this combo family/maternity session early in the morning on July 3rd at Inniswood Metro Gardens in Westerville. We tried to stay out of the sun, but even in the shade, the humidity let us know that the holiday weekend was going to be a hot one. Annina didn't seem phased though. She kept her bottle of acqua nearby for the whole session and ran us in circles to the sounds of "corri, corri, corri!". Did I mention that Annina is super smart and happens to speak Italian? Just like last time, Annina made us work to get some smiles out of her, but they were completely worth it when they did burst through! Happy 4th! Keep smiling! Kaylina
