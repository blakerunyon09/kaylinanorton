2016: A Year in Review

engagements / personal / portraits / weddings

Things have been a little quiet on the blog lately. I wrapped up my sessions for the year at the beginning of the month and have been enjoying some well deserved time off of photography the last couple of weeks. With the year coming to a close though, I thought it was time to break my silence and give one more final thank you to all of the amazing clients I've had the pleasure to work with this year. This year was a little crazy all around and my business saw changes in 2016 that I could not be happier with. In addition to being on target for weddings for the year, the portrait aspect of my business grew 240%! I am so excited to be delving into this new portion of photography - babies and families used to be so intimidating, but now I can honestly say I love and look forward to each session! I'm bad at playing favorites so I apologize for the mass amount of photos in this blog post, but here are my favorite images from the year! Happy New Years! Keep smiling! Kaylina
