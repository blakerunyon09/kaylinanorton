Brian + Abby: Arch Park and North Bank Park Engagement Session | Columbus, Ohio

engagements

Abby and Brian are hands down amazing. Not only are they beautiful people, inside and out, they also were patient enough to do TWO engagement sessions with me. Their first session was rained out in the first half hour but we managed to get some great photos with their dog and some adorable rainy day pics before the sky completely opened on us. So a couple weeks later, we met back up at Arch Park to finish what we started. Abby and Brian got engaged last fall at Milestone 229 so we decided to take their engagement photos a little further down on the Scioto Mile at Arch Park and North Bank Park. I loved getting to know them and hearing all about their relationship as we explored the two parks. They told me about the first time they met (she thought he was cute enough to forget about stranger danger and let him in her car) and their first date (they went to Marcella's and he can still remember what she was wearing). They immediately clicked, and now, they'll be married in just a few short months! Did I mention Abby is STUNNING? We had saved a bottle of champagne to open at the end of the night, but Abby got a little excited and no one was quite ready. This still might be one of my favorite shots though :)Abby and Brian, thank you so much for spending not one, but TWO evenings with me. I had so much fun getting to know you guys and I cannot wait for the wedding! Bring lots of champagne to the wedding, because we will get that picture! Keep smiling! Kaylina
