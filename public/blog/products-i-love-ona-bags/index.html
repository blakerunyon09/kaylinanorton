Products I Love: ONA Bags

for photographers

As a girl, I love good looking bags. Purses, weekenders, duffles, and totes - the more the better. As a photographer, I love a camera bag that can actually hold all the junk I need with me on a wedding day. That's where ONA comes in. ONA makes great looking camera bags that are not only gorgeous, but extremely functional. We recently picked up the Leather Brixton from ONA for Blake to sport on a wedding day, and now I want one for myself! It's amazing what you can fit in this thing! Here's what we can stash inside: A Canon 5d Mark III body with a 24-105mm attached, a 35mm, a 70-200mm, a 600 EX-RT, two extra camera batteries, 8 extra AA batteries, battery chargers for both my camera and my flash batteries, an iPad, SD card reader, business cards, microfiber cloth, a wallet, and an iPhone 6. Two notes: 1) The bag is large enough to fit a 13" Macbook instead of an iPad. We just prefer taking the iPad to weddings. 2) The bag with the above contents weighs about 18.5 pounds. Ouch. So if you're the kind of person who never takes your bag off your body on a wedding day, I would either pack lighter or this might not be the bag for you. ONA bags are real leather so it's prone to some scratches and wear, but that's what makes leather so great - it only gets better with time! This thing is heavy duty - heavy, metal clasps make sure the main cover stays closed when you want it to, but are easy enough to open so you can get in and out of the bag quickly. Photographers, if you're looking for an amazing quality bag that will carry everything you need and look great while doing it - check out ONA! Keep smiling! Kaylina
