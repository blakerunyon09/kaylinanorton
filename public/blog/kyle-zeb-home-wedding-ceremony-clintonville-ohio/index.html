Kyle + Zeb: In-home Wedding Ceremony | Clintonville, Ohio

weddings

I try not to play favorites with the weddings I photograph, but I'm not going to lie - this one is my favorite. Last Saturday my big brother got married! I am so excited and proud of Kyle and Zeb. They tied the knot in a very small, intimate ceremony at their home in Clintonville, Ohio. Before the ceremony, Kyle and Zeb took the time to have some portraits taken. We started at their house and then headed to Stauf's in Grandview where the two had their first date many years ago. After the portrait session and when the guests arrived, Kyle and Zeb made their way to their backyard where they were married by a close friend.  The face you make when you put the ring on the wrong hand... After they said "I do", Kyle and Zeb shared great coffee (Stauf's), amazing food (Aladdin's Eatery), and laughs with the small group of friends and immediate family who were present. Kyle & Zeb - Congratulations! I am so incredibly happy for you two and am so proud to officially have Zeb as part of the family! Keep smiling! Kaylina
