Tony + Madee: Worthington Inn Wedding Columbus, Ohio

first look / weddings

Madee and Tony found each other. In a city of 8.4 million people, Madee and Tony found each other. After a date at a diner near Central Park and a walk that turned in to a run when the rain started, they each knew they had found the one. Shortly after meeting, Tony joined the Air Force and moved to Virginia and Madee moved back home to Ohio to finish school, but when you've found the one - you don't let them go. Saturday was a beautiful February day and the best day ever for Madee and Tony because they were about to begin their lives together. Madee and Tony chose to do a first look in the lobby of the Worthington Inn before their ceremony, and I'm so glad they did! Their nerves and excited jitters immediately calmed as soon as these two laid eyes on each other. Madee recently graduated with a degree in English and is an avid book reader. She did an amazing job integrating the Worthington Inn's history and beautiful architecture with her literature themed decor. The escort cards doubled as a guest book where guests could leave warm wishes and kind words after finding their seats at tables named after Madee's favorite books. Madee and Tony's ceremony was gorgeous and intimate. With Madee's little brother presiding over the ceremony, there was a lot of laughter, a few tears, a kiss, and some cheering. Madee and Tony were married and while the crew at the Worthington Inn transformed the ceremony room into the reception hall, we snuck outside for a few pictures of the happy couple! Madee was absolutely stunning in her dress. She looked like royalty standing in front of this ivy wall with her bouquet, beautiful flowing tulle dress, dark hair, and fair skin. I could not get over just how gorgeous she was! Back inside the Worthington Inn the cocktail hour was in full swing. Madee's eclectic centerpieces matched perfectly with the Inn's romantic decor. Brunch turned into cake cutting which led to Madee and Tony's first dances with each other and then their parents. Madee and Tony - thank you so much for having Blake and I be a part of your big day! It was such an honor to spend the afternoon with you and get to know your friends and family. Seeing the love you two share with each other and your families was heart warming and I can't wait to see what the future holds for you two! Keep smiling! Kaylina
