Sean + Sarah: Baby Announcement Photos - Powell, Ohio

baby / couples / portraits

Baby announcements always make my heart happy, but baby announcements from my past brides and grooms are absolutely the best! I was so excited for Sean and Sarah when Sarah reached out to me earlier this year to see if I'd be interested in shooting a pregnancy announcement for them while they were home for Thanksgiving. This amazing couple got married last July at Cheers Chalet and their intimate ceremony in the woods was to die for! Now that these two have shared the good news to friends and family, it's my turn to share the images! Sean and Sarah met me at Highbanks Metro Park in Powell, Ohio last Saturday for a very quick session to snap some pictures of their exciting news. It was so much fun to see these two again! I loved trying to pose Sarah so her adorable, itty-bitty baby bump would show in the pictures! Sean and Sarah - Congratulations! I am so excited for you two and I cannot wait to meet little Miss Zoë one day! Keep smiling! Kaylina
