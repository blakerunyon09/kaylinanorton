Meet Sid: 2017 High School Senior Portraits - Gahanna, Ohio

portraits / seniors

World, meet Sid! Sid is a Senior at Gahanna Lincoln High School where he plays baseball and is an Eagle Scout. Sid is funny, smart, and charming, and had me laughing his entire session. Fun facts about Sid? He's a yo-yo-ing BOSS and is looking at going to college out of state, maybe Oklahoma State University (or the other OSU as I call it). Long story short, I loved hanging out with Sid last Sunday at Creekside in Gahanna for a quick senior session as the sun set! Sid - Thanks so much for hanging out with me Sunday evening! I had so much fun getting to know you. Best of luck at college next year, I know that wherever you end up, you're going to kill it! Keep smiling! Kaylina
