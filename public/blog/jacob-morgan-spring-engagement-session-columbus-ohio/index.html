Jacob + Morgan: Spring Engagement Session | Columbus, Ohio

engagements

I think it's finally official: Spring is here. With warm weather and color starting to appear on trees, I think everyone's just a little happier. And one of the things that made me happy last weekend was this wonderful engagement session with Jacob and Morgan. These two were so great to get to know as we walked around OSU. Having met through The Ohio State University and now both working there, it was the perfect place to start their spring engagement session. Meeting under the Rotunda at Ohio Stadium, we made our way to the RPAC, Buckeye Grove and along the banks of the Olentangy, snapping pictures along the way.  Looking for some pretty blooms, we headed over to German Village to finish the night's session with some sunshine and pink blossoms. My favorite part about these two? I seriously didn't have to pose them at all - I'd tell them which direction to face and they'd take it from there. They snuggled, kissed, and just looked plain adorable all by themselves! Morgan and Jacob - I am beyond excited for your wedding next June! The next year will fly by and it will be here before you know it! Keep smiling! Kaylina
