Drew + Alison: OSU Maternity Photos

couples / portraits

I had the pleasure of photographing Drew and Alison's wedding last May so I was so excited for them when Alison reached out earlier in the year to let me know that she and Drew were expecting! They're waiting to find out the gender of the baby, so all I can say is that I can't wait to meet him or her! We were originally planning on a fun, football themed maternity session since both Drew and Alison are big Buckeyes fans, but when the weather turned grey and cold, we made a quick change of plans and shot their session on Saturday in Thompson Library on OSU's campus. The library's full of fun nooks and crannies to explore but I was really excited that Drew and Alison were up for braving the cold for a few minutes at the end of the session too! Alison and Drew - thanks so much for spending the morning with me on Saturday! I cannot wait to meet the little one in just a few more weeks! You guys are going to be amazing parents! Keep smiling! Kaylina
