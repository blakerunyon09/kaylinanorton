Jack + Emily: Mouton & Glen Echo Park Engagement Session | Columbus, Ohio

engagements

Jack and Emily are just the perfect, laid-back couple. They're down-to-Earth, genuine people who I seriously had the most fun hanging out with during their engagement session this past fall. I mean, when you start your photo shoot with gorgeous cocktails at an amazing local bar and end your session among incredible fall foliage, I'm not going to have any complaints. Jack and Emily met me at Mouton in the Short North to kick off their engagement session with a little liquid courage. Not that these two needed it at all - they're 100% adorable together and fit together perfectly. After drinks and some great conversation, we headed down to Glen Echo Park in Clintonville to enjoy all the colors the fall leaves had to offer. Jack and Emily were great sports as we explored the park and discussed how one can keep ducks for their eggs. Spoiler alert: we decided that more Googling was required on the subject. I know I've said it before, but I'll say it again - 100% adorable! Jack and Emily - I am so excited for your wedding in August! I can't wait to celebrate your big day! Keep smiling! Kaylina
