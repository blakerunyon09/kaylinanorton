April 9, 2016

Instagram Social: Ohio Creative Collective

for photographers

Last Saturday I had the pleasure of participating in the Ohio Creative Collective's Social Media Social on Instagram. 30 creative boss ladies of Columbus came to the event to learn tips and tricks on how to create a beautiful and effective Instagram feed for their small businesses. Megan Ericson shared some great insights and tips into Instagram as a platform, while myself, Jessy Howard (of The Howard Brand), and Tyanna Green (of Ty Nicole Photography) answered questions about photography and creating a cohesive look for their accounts. The event itself was hosted at Art with Anna's beautiful studio in Bexley. I could not get enough of the beautiful afternoon light pouring through the windows - it was a wonderful morning full of mimosas, some Ridiculously Good Salsa, and making new friends. If you're interested in attending something like this in the future, make sure to follow Ohio Creative Collective on Facebook and Instagram to stay up to date with all of their events! Keep smiling! Kaylina
