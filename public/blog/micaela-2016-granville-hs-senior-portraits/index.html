Micaela: 2016 Granville HS Senior Portraits

portraits / seniors

Let's start with the obvious: Micaela is completely gorgeous. A senior cross country runner at Granville High School, Micaela is full of life and energy and was so much fun to hang out with Sunday evening as we explored German Village during her senior portraits session! She hopes to continue her running career in college, but other than that - she's keeping her options open. Micaela was a natural in front of the camera and never skipped a beat, juggling my questions about her college plans and looking fantastic while doing it!
Seriously, Micaela - you are STUNNING!!
After exploring German Village for a while and getting to know a chatty valet at Lindey's, Micaela and I headed over to Scioto Audubon Park to take advantage of the setting sun and the gorgeous fields of flowers.
Micaela, thanks so much for hanging out with me on Sunday and I hope you love these photos! It was such a pleasure to get to know you! You are such a beautiful girl and I know that wherever you end up and whatever you do, you'll rock at it! Best of luck! Keep smiling! Kaylina
