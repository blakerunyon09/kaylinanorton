Meet Vera: Lifestyle Family Session

baby / family / lifestyle / portraits

Meet Vera. This adorable little girl turned one in August and was such a pleasure to hang out with on Saturday! I absolutely love lifestyle family sessions because they allow me to photograph a family in their home and capture the authentic way they interact. So we started out the morning with Vera finishing up a bottle, reading some books, and heading up stairs to play with mommy's accessories. Vera was a little unsure of the camera so I kept getting some serious looks from her, but when that smile broke out it was all worth it. She's an absolute beauty! It was a beautiful fall day, so we headed outside to play for a little bit. We finished up the session by heading inside and watching a little Sesame Street and dancing along! Keep smiling! Kaylina
