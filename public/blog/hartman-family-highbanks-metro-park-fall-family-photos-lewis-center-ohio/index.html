Hartman Family: Highbanks Metro Park Fall Family Photos | Lewis Center, Ohio

family / portraits

I met the Hartman family at Highbanks Metro Park at the end of October for a quick family portrait session. I have to admit I was a littler nervous going into the session because it was the first truly cold weekend of the year. Knowing that little girls in adorable summer dresses aren't exactly fans for 40 degree weather, Jesse and I had already mentally prepared ourselves for less than full cooperation. But these amazing little girls did such a great job! Snuggles, tickles, and giggles were all they needed to keep happy faces for the camera. Love this adorable little family for turning a chilly morning into the perfect day! Keep smiling! Kaylina
