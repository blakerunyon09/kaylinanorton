Emma Turns 1: Creekside Gahanna Family Portraits | Columbus, Ohio

baby / family / portraits

I cannot believe that little Miss Emma is about to turn 1. It feels like just yesterday we shot her newborn pictures! Emma is now pulling herself up to stand, has the most adorable little giggle, and LOVES ice cream. We shot Emma's one year portraits at Creekside Plaza in Gahanna. We explored the park, the water, the woods, and most importantly, the nearby Coldstone Creamery for an impromptu ice cream smash to celebrate Emma's first birthday. This is what I call sisterly love... Love these faces! She's too cute! Keep smiling! Kaylina Check out these posts to see Emma's first year in pictures!
