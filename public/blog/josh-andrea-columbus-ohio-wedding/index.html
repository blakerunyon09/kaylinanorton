October 14, 2015

Josh + Andrea: Columbus, Ohio Wedding

weddings

Close your eyes and imagine the perfect fall day. The sun is shining, there is a soft breeze rustling the newly changed leaves, birds are singing, and there's a tangible buzz of excitement as people gather to celebrate. This time we weren't all coming together to cheer on the Buckeyes (although they did win last Saturday) - Josh and Andrea were blessed with the most perfect autumn day for their wedding last Saturday as their friends and family gathered at Raymond Memorial Golf Course in Columbus, Ohio to cheer them on as they tied the knot. We started the day with both sides of the bridal party getting ready at Raymond Memorial Golf Course's Reception House. After hair and make-up were in place and Josh and Andrea exchanged their gifts (and shed a few tears), Andrea slipped into her dress and we headed outside for some portraits of just her and the girls. Andrea - you are gorgeous!! The ceremony was held behind Raymond Memorial's Reception House and the golf course made a beautiful background for the happy couple as they exchanged rings and vows. After the ceremony we took a short drive on a golf cart to a nearby bridge and Josh and Andrea had a few moments to themselves before the reception began. At the end of night Josh and Andrea made a grand exit with sparklers and kissed under a beautiful October sky. Josh and Andrea - Your wedding day was absolutely perfect from beginning to end and Blake and I are so thankful to have been a part of it! Thank you so much for including us on your big day! Keep smiling! Kaylina Thank you to the following vendors who made Josh and Andrea's day even more special!
