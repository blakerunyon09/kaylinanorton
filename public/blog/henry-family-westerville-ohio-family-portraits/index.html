Henry Family: Westerville, Ohio Family Portraits

family / kids / portraits

Summer is the best time of the year. My Saturdays are full of weddings and my Sundays are full of adorable little ones with the most amazing parents. Last weekend I spent my Sunday morning with the Henry family and had so much fun getting to know Sydney and her mom and dad! Sydney has beautiful curls and big brown eyes, but most importantly, she's smart, inquisitive, and such a delight to work with! Getting her warmed up to the camera was a little work, but once we turned on the Moana soundtrack, we were in business. :) We shot this great, lifestyle family photo session last Sunday morning at Everal Barn & Homestead in Westerville, Ohio. This is one of my favorite locations in Columbus for family pictures because it is such a pretty location with the farm house, pond, and weeping willow. It also has shade at all times of the day which makes it a photographer's dream! But mostly I love it because it's secluded and never very busy, so we can take out time and linger on shots and settings we like without feeling rushed. Because Lord knows anything you can do to remove stress from family portraits is worth it! Love her natural smile! So beautiful! And these lashes! My, oh, my! Keep smiling! Kaylina
