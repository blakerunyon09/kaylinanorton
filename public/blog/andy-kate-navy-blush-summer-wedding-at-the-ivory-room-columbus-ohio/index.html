Andy + Kate: Navy & Blush Summer Wedding at the Ivory Room | Columbus, Ohio

sunset session / weddings

Saturday may have been one of the hottest days of the year in Columbus, but it was an absolutely perfect day for Kate & Andy to get married. From the morning at the Westin downtown, to their beautiful ceremony at St. Agatha's in Upper Arlington, to bridal party pictures outside of Ohio Stadium, to a gorgeous sunset outside of the Ivory Room along the Scioto Mile, Kate & Andy's big day took us all over Columbus and it was - perfect. Before the ceremony, Andy gifted Kate with a beautiful, diamond necklace whose center stone was a family heirloom.   Kate and Andy got married at the gorgeous St. Agatha's church in Upper Arlington. After the ceremony, Kate & Andy loaded up the entire bridal party in the party bus and headed off to Ohio Stadium. While there was construction blocking must of the rotunda, we still managed to get in the spirit for a few minutes before heading over to the Scioto Mile for more pictures.   Before you think that we're spelling challenged and can't spell OHIO - Andy and Kate are the brand new Mr. & Mrs. Hoh. After first dances and as the sun was setting, Andy & Kate snuck outside for some sunset photos and snuggles before heading back inside to party the night away. Kate & Andy - thank you so much for having me be a part of your perfect day! I loved traveling around Columbus with you and partying with the rest of the bridal party! You guys are so much fun and I hope you have an amazing time on your honeymoon! Enjoy every minute of Italy! Keep smiling!
