David + Sarah: Ohio State University Engagement Photos | Columbus, Ohio

engagements

Sarah and David got engaged this past winter on a bench on the Oval at Ohio State University. David had it all planned out, they'd walk around campus together and he'd pop the question at the Whispering Wall at the end of the Oval, by the Wexner Center for the Arts. There was one problem: it was insanely cold and trying to casually convince Sarah to go on a seemingly mindless walk was a little difficult. So he adapted, and ended up asking Sarah to become his wife on a bench near University Hall at the other end of the Oval. And in the end, the exact location didn't matter, because Sarah and David are engaged and they both describe that day as perfect. For Sarah and David's engagement session last Sunday, we met at Thompson Library on OSU's campus to explore the oval, head down to the Wexner Center, and take advantage of the beauty of the Horseshoe. It was a perfect fall night and I had so much fun getting to know Sarah and David. They are so much fun to be around and I cannot wait for their wedding next September in Cleveland. Keep smiling! Kaylina
