Corey + Sami: 94th Aero Squadron Engagement Session - Columbus, Ohio

engagements

Corey and Sami recently celebrated 5 years together. Next June they'll say "I do" and multiply those 5 years by forever. We met up for their engagement session on Sunday at the 94th Aero Squadron near Port Columbus International Airport. In addition to their dogs, Sami and Corey love old airplanes and whiskey barrels. So the 94th Aero Squadron was the perfect place to make it all come together. The wind was blowing, the sun was shining, and Corey, Sami, and their adorable dogs were just too cute to handle. Sami and Corey - thanks so much for spending the evening with me! You guys were so much fun to shoot! Sami, you are absolutely gorgeous and you're going to make one badass bride! I cannot wait for your wedding in June - I know it's going to be one of a kind! Keep smiling! Kaylina
