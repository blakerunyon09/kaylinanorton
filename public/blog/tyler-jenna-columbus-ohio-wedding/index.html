Tyler + Jenna: Columbus, Ohio Wedding

weddings

Jenna and Tyler have an adorable awkwardness about them. They love to joke around and have fun. They may not be your typical can't-keep-their-hands-off-each-other couple, but anyone who sees them interacting with each other can see that their relationship is built on friendship and laughter. Like their relationship, their wedding on Saturday at Xenos Christian Fellowship was full of that same spirit. It was absolutely magical and refreshing. Jenna and a few of her bridesmaids started the day at Virtue Salon in Clintonville for hair and make-up. Virtue, a vegan concept salon, had a unique atmosphere and did an amazing job catering to the girls' needs.






After the salon, us girls headed to the church to finish getting ready while the boys got ready a few rooms over. Neither Tyler nor Jenna were nervous and both had a lot of fun enjoying the morning with friends before the ceremony.






We set up an impromptu first look for Jenna's dad...

and there wasn't a dry eye in the house afterward.





Before the ceremony started Jenna's bridesmaids and family prayed for her and the life she was about to begin with Tyler.

The ceremony itself was short and sweet. Exactly what I like. The pastor made a few jokes, Tyler and Jenna looked madly in love, and there were a few happy tears from the moms. Who could ask for more?





Sometimes the moment before the first kiss is the best part...



After the ceremony we rounded up the family and bridal party and ventured outside for some group pictures. After all of the family pictures and bridal party pictures, I figured Tyler and Jenna would be ready to run back to their party but they were good sports (especially considering it was 86 degrees out) and let me snap just a few more of them.








Then it was time to party!















I love catching small moments like this on the dance floor...



Jenna and Tyler- thank you so much for having Blake and I tag along with you on your special day! It was so great to get to know you guys and your friends and family! I hope you're having an amazing time on your honeymoon! Keep smiling! Kaylina
