Goldsmith Family Photos: Jeffrey Mansion - Bexley, Ohio

baby / family / portraits

This past Sunday I had the pleasure of photographing the amazing Goldsmith family at Jeffrey Mansion in Bexley, Ohio! It always so much fun to get to know new clients, and spending the morning with 3 year old Sloan and 1 year old Grier and their amazing parents was no exception. As we explored the grounds of Jeffrey Mansion, Sloan filled me in on her very exciting life and I quickly learned that not only did I love Sloan, but their whole family! The Goldsmiths all dressed up as Star Wars characters for Halloween and Sloan was very proud of her Han Solo costume. A family who nerds out together always gets a few extra cool points in my book. Getting a 1 year old and a 3 year old to smile for the camera wore us adults out. Their full gallery will include a lot of bloopers and a few crying faces, which I think is half the fun! I think ultimately everyone had a good time and the pictures turned out adorable, but I'll let you be the judge! Keep smiling! Kaylina
