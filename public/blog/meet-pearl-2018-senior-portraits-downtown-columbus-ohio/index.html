Meet Pearl: 2018 Senior Portraits | Downtown Columbus, Ohio

portraits / seniors

Last weekend's senior session with Pearl was absolute perfection. This gorgeous girl and her mom drove up from Virginia to have her senior pictures taken and I could not be more thankful that they took the time to meet me up here in beautiful Columbus, Ohio. The weather was perfect and Pearl was beyond stunning in every single one of her outfits. Shout out to Jess at Studio Fovero in German Village who did an amazing job on Pearl's makeup for the portrait session! Pearl was such a joy to work with. She's sweet and so smart and her confidence in front of a camera is down right enviable. Pearl will be graduating this spring and although she doesn't have her plans after high school solidified, her interest in art has her leaning towards a degree in design. I have no doubt that whatever she ends up pursuing, she'll kill it because she's seriously just a boss. Hands down. Okay, can we just talk about how jealous I am of Pearl's hair? Seriously, the wind can blow in any direction and this girl looks like a straight up super model. Seriously, girl - hair goals. Keep smiling! Kaylina
