Sarah: 2016 Granville HS Senior Portraits

portraits / seniors

Here's what you need to know about Sarah: She's just recently started her senior year at Granville High School and is seriously one of the sweetest and coolest girls I've ever met. Sarah plays tennis, hangs out with friends, goes to awesome concerts, and of course, knows how to relax with a good TV show on Netflix. Sarah's planning on heading to OSU next fall to pursue an EEDS major which basically means she'll be a bonafide sustainability superhero. Oh, and did I mention she's absolutely gorgeous? We shot Sarah's senior pictures at Denison University in Granville, Ohio last weekend and I had so much fun exploring the beautiful campus with Sarah. We started out with the "boring" yearbook photos and even those she killed! After exploring Denison a little more and an outfit change, we found this gorgeous light and Sarah continued to be an absolute super model. I loved every single one of the outfits Sarah brought to her session. Fashionable yet laid back. She looked beautiful in every single one of them! Another "boring" shot that Sarah rocked out... Sarah- Thank you, thank you, thank you for letting me take your senior pictures! You were a complete pro in front of the camera and I seriously cannot get over how gorgeous you are! I hope you had as much fun getting your pictures taken as I had taking them! I cannot wait to see what the next few years have in store for you, but I know without a doubt you'll kick butt wherever you find yourself in the future! And enjoy your senior year - it will be over before you know it! Keep smiling! Kaylina
