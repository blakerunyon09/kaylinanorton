Seth + Amy: Watersedge Wedding Hilliard, Ohio

first look / sunset session / weddings

From the moment I stepped into Amy's hotel room Friday morning, I knew she and Seth were going to have the most relaxed wedding of the year. Looking completely gorgeous and zen at the same time, Amy's calm vibes in the morning carried through the rest of the day and made for an amazing wedding that was laid back and oh, so much fun! Seth and Amy chose to do a first look at their venue, Watersedge, in Hilliard and this shaded path along the edge of the pond was the perfect location for them to see each other for the first time as bride and groom. After their first look, we took a couple of minutes to shoot some portraits of just the two of them before the festivities of the day kicked off. And let me just say, these two together just make my heart melt. After their pictures together it was finally time for the main event: the ceremony! It was a beautiful ceremony that felt like an intimate, backyard gathering with their closest friends and family coming together to celebrate Seth and Amy's love. While dinner was being served, Seth and Amy snuck outside to dance next to the water as the sun dipped behind the trees. Now it was officially party time! Seth and Amy, thank you, thank you, thank you for letting Blake and I tag along with you on your special day! You guys seriously win the award for most laid back couple and I hope your honeymoon is just as relaxed and enjoyable as your wedding was! You two are amazing and I wish the best for you for all the years to come! Keep smiling!
