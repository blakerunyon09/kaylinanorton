Ryan + Emily: Autumn Engagement Session Granville

engagements

Emily and Ryan's first dates could be described as the "Dating Olympics" - sports or athletic filled adventures that didn't always go as planned (like Emily falling down and hitting her head while ice skating). But when Ryan went to go get ice for her head and Emily handled the fall with as much grace as possible- these two knew they had found something special. Ryan and Emily are the snuggliest couple I've ever met. They're so snuggly, I had to make up the word "snuggliest". We started out our session on Sunday in front of The Buxton Inn in Granville, Ohio and explored their grounds and the nearby streets. When we found our first location, I instructed them to sit down on this bench and just relax while I adjusted my camera settings. Luckily my camera settings were already pretty good because this is what they did without any instruction from me...
Pure magic. These two are amazing. For the rest of the session all I had to do was sit or stand Emily and Ryan near each other and they took care of the rest. They interact and love each other so naturally that my only job was to capture the romance.
A mustard colored wall has never looked so good.
I love, love, LOVE the autumn colors Granville had to offer us on Sunday. These trees and leaves combined with Ryan and Emily's perfect fall garb were making me a very happy photographer.
Ryan and Emily, thank you so much for spending Sunday with me. I had such a great time walking around Granville with the two of you and capturing your love. I am so excited for your wedding in May- I know that it is going to be another incredible day full of love and snuggling! Keep smiling! Kaylina Update: Ryan and Emily are married!
