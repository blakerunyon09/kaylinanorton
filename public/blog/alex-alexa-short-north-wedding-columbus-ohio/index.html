Alex + Alexa: Short North Wedding Columbus, Ohio

sunset session / weddings

Oh you guys, sometimes being at a wedding just reinvigorates you! Being around Alex, Alexa, and their families last Saturday was such an amazing experience. Alex and Alexa are two of the sweetest people you will ever meet. They're such incredible souls who love books and adventure almost as much as they love each other. Their wedding on Saturday at the Immaculate Conception Catholic Church in Clintonville was beautiful and their reception at The Annunciation Greek Orthodox Cathedral in the Short North was the perfect party to end the night. After the ceremony, Alex, Alexa, and their bridal party made their way to Goodale Park for some portraits via trolley. After pictures in the park, we headed to Brickle St. in the Short North for some fun photos in the alley. Then it was back to the trolley for a quick stop at the nearby bar LEVEL and then off to the reception for the real party! Alex and Alexa - I cannot thank you enough for letting Blake and I photograph your special day. It was such an honor to be your guests as you said "I do" and started the next chapter of your love story. I hope you guys are having the most magnificent time in Portugal on your honeymoon - you deserve the absolute best! Keep smiling!
