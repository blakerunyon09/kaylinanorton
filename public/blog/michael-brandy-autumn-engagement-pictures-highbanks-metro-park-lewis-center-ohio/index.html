Michael + Brandy: Autumn Engagement Pictures Highbanks Metro Park | Lewis Center, Ohio

engagements

Michael and Brandy just might be the cutest couple in the world. When we met at Highbanks Metro Park on Monday evening for their engagement session, I was so excited to see the leaves in their full autumn colors. And when I commented that not only their outfits, but Michael's beard was coordinated for fall colors, Brandy said, "my favorite person and my favorite season". They spent the rest of the evening snuggling and just being down right adorable. They made my job incredibly easy and I loved spending every moment with them. I seriously cannot wait for their wedding in February, it will be here before we know it! Brandy and Michael - thanks so much for taking time out of your week to explore Highbanks with me! You two are the cutest and I cannot wait to capture the moment you become husband and wife! Keep smiling Kaylina
