Kevin + Allison: Couple Session

couples / portraits

Allison and Kevin are about the cutest couple you'll ever meet. I had such a great time shooting their wedding last year. So when Allison contacted me asking me to take some very exciting pictures for she and Kevin a few weeks ago, I couldn't resist.

We started out warm and cozy by the fire but soon headed outside to brave the cold and make the big announcement: Kevin and Allison will be welcoming their first baby in July 2014.
I could not be happier for this amazing couple and, as if the universe felt the same way, we were blessed with a mini snow storm to add some extra cuteness to our shoot.
And yes, we just had to do some cheesy announcement photos because who could resist?
Congratulations Kevin and Allison- you guys are going to make such amazing, loving parents (and a very cute baby)! Keep smiling! Kaylina
