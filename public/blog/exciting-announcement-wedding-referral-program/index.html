Exciting Announcement: 2016 Wedding Referral Program

engagements / for clients / portraits / tips / weddings

We're officially a week into 2016, and I think it's the perfect time to announce something that I've been working on for a while... I always maintain that I have the best clients and my business would be absolutely nothing without them. Referrals and word of mouth are the best compliment I can receive and I can never thank people enough for passing on a good word about me, but I can definitely try! If you refer a couple to me for wedding photography services and they book before the end of the year, I'll say "THANK YOU" by giving you a completely complimentary portrait session! This portrait session can be used for anything! Family photos, an anniversary session with your better half, a lifestyle session documenting your family in their Saturday morning best, or you can get dressed up and pose with your dog. The possibilities are limitless! The free session would be a 45 minute shoot at a location of your choosing within an hour of Columbus, you can have up to two outfits if you're interested in getting fancy, and you'll receive the digital files from your shoot delivered via an online gallery! The only stipulation is you use your free session within a year of your referral! I'll reach out as soon as your referral has booked their wedding to talk details! You don't have to be a past client to take advantage of this offer. If you're just a friendly face who has supported my work from afar, you're eligible for a free session too! I don't want this post to get too sappy, but I am honestly blown away from the support of everyone I come across. I've been chasing this photography dream for 5 years now and every day I'm surprised by a new person who knows about my work and offers encouragement in ways I've never imagined. Yes, this referral program is a way for me to hopefully grow my business over the next year, but it's also about showing my appreciation to the people who have helped me get to the place I am and to the people who are helping me go further. So thank you, from the bottom of my heart. I can't wait to share the rest of this journey with you! Keep smiling! Kaylina P.S. If you have any questions about this offer or would just like to talk about your favorite new Netflix obsession - fill out the contact form on KaylinaNorton.com and I'll do my best to respond right away!
