Kiran + Sara: Downtown Columbus, Ohio Engagement Shoot

engagements

Sometimes your friends know what's best for you. Even if you don't. That was definitely the case when it came to Kiran and Sara finding their soul mates. Though Kiran and Sara continued to bump into each other in life, they never really clicked until mutual friends convinced them they were perfect for each other. Just like that, they found their lives transformed. And in less than a month, Kiran and Sara will find themselves newlyweds! Let me start by saying that Kiran and Sara are not procrastinators. Waiting until they were 4 weeks out from their wedding to take their engagement pictures was never their original plan, but Ohio weather took its toll this spring. After postponing our session twice, the pressure was on - it was this Saturday or never. Luckily, Mother Nature came through for us and gave us an absolutely gorgeous day to explore Columbus, and Kiran and Sara were ready for it. Kiran and Sara hopped out of their car looking dropdead gorgeous - these two know how to rock an outfit. We started off their session downtown on the Scioto Mile's beautiful Main Street Bridge then explored the area around Cosi before heading over to the Arena District to Firestone Alley as the sun set. These two were so much fun to photograph and they looked great in every single locale! Sara and Kiran - thank you so much for spending your Saturday evening with me! It was so much fun to explore Columbus with you and I cannot wait for the wedding! It will be here before you know it! Keep smiling!
