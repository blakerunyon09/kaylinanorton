Steve + Caley: Couples Session Millersport, Ohio

couples / portraits

World, meet Steve and Caley. Or, as I call them, Dad and Mom. They've been married for 34 years next month and haven't had pictures taken of them in almost as long. I've had my business up and running for over 5 years now and somehow have never taken pictures for my parents. So a couple of weeks ago, I fixed that. We did their session at their home in Millersport, Ohio while the leaves were still on the trees and the Indian Summer was in full effect. It was so much fun to spend 30 minutes with my parents walking around their land and showing off what I love to do. I'm so happy to be able to give them updated pictures together. Now here's hoping it doesn't take another 30 years for some more! Keep smiling! Kaylina
