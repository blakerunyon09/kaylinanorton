Andy + Kate: Scioto Mile Spring Engagement Portraits | Columbus, Ohio

engagements

Let's start by making it official, Kate and Andy win the bravest couple of the year award. Hopefully you can't tell from the photos, but Kate and Andy faced an outdoor engagement session in 38 degree weather along the windy Scioto Mile. They started the session with some liquid encouragement in the form of cocktails at Milestone 229 before heading outside. Kate looked gorgeous in her dress and was amazing at making me believe she wasn't freezing to death during the shoot. Luckily, the Scioto Mile had some beautiful blossoms on display for us, so even if it was freezing in real life, the photos look like spring had actually sprung! Kate and Andy - thank you so much for braving the cold with me! You two are so much fun and if you don't mind me saying, absolutely adorable together! I cannot wait for your wedding in June - here's hoping it will be about 35 degrees warmer! :) Keep smiling! Kaylina
