Wheeler Family: In-Home Family Pictures | Powell, Ohio

baby / family / lifestyle / portraits

The Wheeler family welcomed me into their home last November for a family session that was full of fun and furry friends. Kristen, Andy, and 3 month old Graham, were the perfect models and in true lifestyle session fashion, we played the entire session by ear. We started in the living room and played with Parker (the dog), headed towards a changing station when Graham got a little fussy, and ended up in Graham's beautiful nursery. (Seriously, can someone please decorate my bedroom to match?) All along the way, I snapped candids of this amazing family just enjoying a weekend day together. They were probably a little more put-together than normal, but I hope they can look back on this session as a glimpse of what life together was like at this stage in their lives. Spoiler alert: it's adorable ;) And I always love ending family sessions with just a few pictures of mom and dad! It's important to continue taking pictures together - just the two of you! Keep smiling! Kaylina
