Brent + Nicole: German Village Engagement Session

engagements

Boy meets girl, boy chases girl, girl plays hard to get, boy buys girl plane tickets to Vegas to impress her, they fall in love. It's a tale as old as time. Brent and Nicole are fun people. They like to joke around, give each other a hard time, and tease each other. But any one who has the pleasure of interacting with them can see that beneath the playful jokes is a massive amount of love. They've been together for "a long time" and have been engaged for  three years. They've loved, been through hard times, laughed, started a family together, and this fall, they will tie the knot. These two are meant to be together and I couldn't be happier to see such a well matched couple get married in October. We met Wednesday night at Schiller Park in German Village for a quick engagement session during sunset. Normally it takes a while for a couple to warm up in front of the camera but Nicole was on it right away.
Absolutely stunning.
After we spent some time in the park we wandered around some of the streets right off of Schiller Park and took advantage of the gorgeous houses that German Village is famous for.
Absolutely adore the way these two interacted.
Nicole's not the only model in this relationship! Look at Brent working it! But then she stole the show again...
We ended the session on a strong note with Brent impressing us with his muscles and willingness to repeatedly lift Nicole up so we could get this beautiful picture.
Thank you Nicole and Brent for being such great sports during your session and doing every crazy thing I asked! You two were amazing and I can not wait to photograph your guys' special day in October! Keep smiling! Kaylina
