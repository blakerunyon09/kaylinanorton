Lifestyle Baby Portraits: Everleigh at 8 months

baby / lifestyle / portraits

This precious baby girl has officially stolen my heart. I first met her last September when she was just 11 days old for a fun lifestyle session in her brand new home. Then I had the pleasure to photograph her under the Christmas tree for baby's first Christmas photos. Now Miss Everleigh is almost 8 months old and I cannot believe how fast she's growing! For her 8 month portraits we decided to venture outside the home and do the shoot at Columbus's Park of Roses. It's one of my favorite parks to shoot at in Columbus, because, regardless of the time of year, it always has some very cute spots to snap photos! We set Everleigh down on a blanket and let her be her adorable self - and she did not disappoint. I cannot get enough of this amazing family! So cute! Everleigh was not so sure about the chair at first, but it ended up winning her over. These pictures with her mommy and daddy make my heart melt. After a little more rolling around on the ground, we ended the session in momma's arms - because that's where a baby girl should be. Keep smiling! Kaylina
