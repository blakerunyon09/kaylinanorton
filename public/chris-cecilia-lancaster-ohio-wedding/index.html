Chris + Cecilia : Lancaster, Ohio Wedding

weddings

She was so nervous for their first date she had to drink some courage in the form of Jameson and Coke. Almost three years later, she poured the same drink for herself, her bridesmaids, and her mom as they toasted the upcoming wedding and prepared to slip her into her beautiful white dress. Cecilia's and Chris's wedding last Saturday was full of small, personal details like that one. The tractor that played a large part in Chris's proposal, a letter to his departed mother, and photos detailing their lives until their wedding day were also all incorporated into the reception. Chris and Cecilia are the picture of the perfect couple and their wedding last weekend was a picture perfect wedding. Both Chris and Cecilia have small town roots with close ties to their families which made for an intimate, fun, and romantic country wedding. Chris and Cecilia- thank you so much for letting Blake and I capture your special day and tell your love story. We are so blessed for having been included in your wedding day. I hope you have an amazing time in Hawaii next week and I know you two have an incredible future ahead of you. Keep smiling! Kaylina We started the day at Studio Fovero in Columbus's gorgeous German Village and then headed back to Cecilia's childhood home to get her in her dress and take a few pictures. Including an adorable one with her furry best friend.


The ceremony and reception were held on land that Cecilia owns not far from where she grew up. She and Chris did an amazing job transforming a field into a gorgeous wedding venue (along with help from Personal Touch Party Rentals).





I loved catching these moments right after Chris and Cecilia walked back down the aisle as a married couple. Cecilia getting a high five from her niece and Chris not being able to keep himself away from his beautiful bride.





Did I mention the amazing flowers and signs!? They were absolutely wonderful and the gorgeous flowers are from the fabulous Madison House Designs.





After such a gorgeous ceremony we spent the rest of the evening eating, laughing, and being led through the rest of the festivities by the amazing Capitol City Sound.


And I have to end with this precious picture from Chris and Cecilia's first dance as a married couple. It's a perfect way to show how close and in love these two were that night and will be for many, many years to come.
