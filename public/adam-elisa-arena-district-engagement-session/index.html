Adam + Elisa: Arena District Engagement Session

engagements

Adam and Elisa met through work. From DC to London to Columbus, Ohio, they never let the distance come between them and now they're ready to become husband and wife. We met at Arch Park in the Arena District for some fun, casual engagement pictures. I absolutely love the way that Elisa and Adam interact. It took some time for Adam to warm up to the camera but Elisa helped bring out the smiles and laughter in everyone. While Elisa and Adam made a wardrobe change I got a chance to photograph Elisa's gorgeous engagement ring. It's absolutely beautiful! Next, we headed down to North Bank Park to get a glimpse of the Columbus skyline. The Scioto Mile might be under construction, but these two can make anything look good. Back at Arch Park with their final outfit change, we raced the setting sun to snap a few last pictures. Promising Adam we only had 5 poses left got a few real smiles out of him and honestly, they're some of my favorites.   Adam and Elisa - thank you so much for spending an evening with me! I loved hanging out with you two and seeing the fun, playful way you love each other. I hope you love the sneak peek and I can't wait to share the whole gallery with you! Keep smiling! Kaylina Update: Adam and Elisa are married! Check out their Licking County Courthouse Wedding!
