Olivia & Henry: In-home Kids Lifestyle Portraits | Bexley, Ohio

family / lifestyle / portraits

Can I just start out by saying that I had so much fun with Olivia and Henry at their house last Saturday? It was a little rainy and grey out and Henry might not have been super, duper excited when I first arrived, but eventually I think I won him over and we got a lot of great pictures. In home lifestyle portraits are my absolute favorite. Getting to take pictures of kids in their homes where they're comfortable always lead to the best pictures. Yes, we take some formal "smile for the camera shots" but it's more about getting natural, candid pictures of them being their goofy selves. And Olivia and Henry were all about that! About half way through the session, when the sun finally poked its head out from behind the grey clouds, we headed outside for some pictures. But more importantly - a tire swing! Keep smiling! Kaylina
