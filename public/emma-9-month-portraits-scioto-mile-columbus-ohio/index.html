Emma 9 Month Portraits | Scioto Mile Columbus, Ohio

baby / family / portraits

Every time I have the pleasure of checking in with this adorable family, it is so much fun. Emma and Addison are beautiful little girls that take after their gorgeous momma. And watching how much they love their daddy always brings a smile to my face. I've been taking pictures of this amazing family for the past year and last weekend's family portrait session was the perfect way to welcome Spring to Columbus, Ohio. We met for little Miss Emma's 9 month baby portraits at COSI on the Scioto Mile Sunday evening. I absolutely loved the perfectly curated outfits Nicole put together for her family. It seriously doesn't get any cuter than this! Keep smiling! Kaylina
