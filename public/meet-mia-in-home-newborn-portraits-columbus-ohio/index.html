Meet Mia: In-Home Newborn Portraits | Columbus, Ohio

baby / lifestyle / portraits

It's amazing how quickly time flies. I first met this amazing family when they were a family of two with one on the way, and now I'm photographing the arrival of their newest bundle of joy: Mia Grace! Mia is one month old and already so loved by her mommy, daddy, older brother Howdy, and devoted guard dog Izzy. Their lifestyle family session this Sunday at their home in Clintonville was so much fun. Howdy kept us all on our toes, Mia refused to sleep, and Izzy disapprovingly kept an eye on me the whole time. I love all the candids we took documenting the day as Julie and Chris showed off their skills at being parents of two! Love all of her little faces! Howdy wasn't a fan of sitting for family pictures, but he was more than happy to pose on his own. Seriously, such a cute family! Keep smiling! Kaylina
