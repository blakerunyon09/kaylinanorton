Joe + Julie: Inniswood Gardens Engagement Shoot

engagements

It was a little hot and muggy Sunday night at Inniswood Metro Gardens in Westerville, Ohio. We had been keeping our eye on the weather for almost a week and were worried about having to reschedule due to rain, but the rain held off and Joe and Julie dealt with the humidity like champs. We spent the evening casually strolling through Inniswood Gardens, stopping when we found a particularly gorgeous spot to take pictures. I loved how comfortable they are interacting with each other. It was so easy to pose them or, more accurately, let them pose themselves. Julie was such a natural and looked so cute in every picture - her smile is so contagious. And take a look at this gorgeous ring! Julie gave Joe all the credit for picking it out himself. He did an amazing job. Joe and Julie have been together for a while and have an adorable baby girl who tagged along to their session and even jumped in on some pictures at the end. I don't think I've ever met a more well behaved and laid back 10 month old! We ended the night with an outfit change and the sun setting over an open field and I adored how Joe and Julie melted in to each other for these last few pictures. Absolutely breathtaking. Joe and Julie - thank you so much for spending the evening with me! I had so much fun exploring Inniswood with you and I cannot wait for the wedding in July! You two are the sweetest couple and I'm so excited to be able to capture your love story on camera! Keep smiling! Kaylina Update: Joe and Julie are married!
