Tyler + Emily: New Albany High School Senior Portraits

portraits / seniors

I shot Tyler and Emily's senior portraits in October during one of the last truly warm days of the fall. Tyler and Emily are twins who will be graduating from New Albany High School in the spring. We began their senior picture session on New Albany High School's sprawling campus then headed over to Creekside Plaza in Gahanna. At Creekside, Tyler and Emily's family dogs jumped in on a few pictures and they did a great job humoring their mom with all of the poses she asked for (like all good children do). Tyler and Emily - thanks so much for waking up early on a weekend to smile for the camera. You guys were great and so much fun! I hope your senior year is amazing and best of luck in the future! Keep smiling! Kaylina
