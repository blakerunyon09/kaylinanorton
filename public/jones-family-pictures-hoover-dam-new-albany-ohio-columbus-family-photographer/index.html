Jones Family Pictures: Hoover Dam, New Albany Ohio - Columbus Family Photographer

family / portraits

Let me tell you something about Miss Morgan. She is a bundle of fun! We met up Saturday morning at Hoover Dam Reservoir Park in New Albany, Ohio for a quick family session and I quickly learned that although she has a dog, Morgan's a budding cat lady. I was instantly drawn in by her glittery kitten flats and the fact she was a kitty cat for Halloween. We spent the rest of the morning learning about what she would name a kitten if she ever got one (Sparkles) and what said kitty's favorite vegetable and fruit would be (carrots and grapes). Morgan and her Mommy and Daddy were so much fun to hang out with. We started out in the woods at the Hoover Dam where, according to Morgan, a very large beaver left a teepee for us. While I'm not sure that's how this structure actually appeared, I appreciated the imagination and enthusiasm for it. After exploring the woods a little more, we emerged from the trees and found a beautiful area where the fall leaves were in full effect. Here, Morgan posed with her mommy and daddy and then we had some fun and got a little silly. The silliness culminated in a tickle fight of epic proportions. When the dust had settled from the tickle fight we took a few more family pictures and ended the day on a fun note with everyone following directions from Morgan as she told us what faces to make in each picture. Keep smiling! Kaylina
