Goldsmith Family: Downtown Library Family Portraits | Columbus, Ohio

family / kids / portraits

The Goldsmith Family is also so much fun to photograph every fall. We meet up shortly after Halloween and I get the scoop on the year's costumes (Ghosbusters) and what's the best candy (all candy). The girls aren't huge fans of pictures, which makes the job a little more challenging, but the candid interactions I get of them, makes it worth all the effort. This year we met at the Main Columbus Metropolitan Library with plans to actually take the pictures inside for fear of bad weather, but as luck would have it, the weather turned out great and the library was closed! So we shot the entire session out front of the library and explored some of the side streets nearby. I'll be coming back to Ohio this fall to shoot family portraits and I cannot wait to see these girls and how much they've grown! Keep smiling! Kaylina Don't forget to check out last year's fall family session at Jeffrey Mansion with the Goldsmith girls to see how they've grown!
