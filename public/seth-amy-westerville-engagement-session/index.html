Seth + Amy: Westerville Engagement Session

engagements

Seth and Amy just have an ease about them. They fit together perfectly and compliment each other in the best ways. At their engagement shoot Sunday at Inniswood Gardens in Westerville, Ohio, Seth and Amy made every frame look effortless and fun. And I couldn't love them more for it. I'm apologizing in advance for channeling my inner Tyra and fangirling over this gorgeous couple. Inniswood is Westerville's most popular park when it comes to photo shoots, and this past Sunday was no exception. As we started our shoot, you could look in any direction and see photographers roaming the park's gorgeous gardens looking for a open place to take their clients. Seth and Amy were great sports as we wandered around the park looking for our own photo ops, but these two look amazing together no matter where you take them.
This was the moment I found out Amy was ticklish and I warned both of them I was going to take advantage of that later in the session.
I loved the height difference with these two - she fits perfectly in his arms!
After an outfit change we headed around to the west side of the park for more open spaces and to catch the last golden light from the setting sun.
And here come the tickles...
Seriously, could these two be a better looking couple?! I teased them all night they'd been practicing their model moves because they are fierce!
And the way she looks at him when he pushes her hair behind her ear - I'm dying over here. Amy, you are too gorgeous!!
The last few frames of their engagement session might be my favorite. Who knew Westerville could be so pretty?
Seth and Amy - thank you so, so much for spending the evening with me on Sunday! I had such a great time taking your engagement pictures and getting to know you two! You guys are so perfect for each other and I am so excited for your wedding next August! Keep smiling! Kaylina
