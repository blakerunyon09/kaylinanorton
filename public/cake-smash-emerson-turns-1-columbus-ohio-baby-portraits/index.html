Cake Smash: Emerson Turns 1 - Columbus, Ohio Baby Portraits

baby / portraits

What can I say about little Miss Emerson? She has big blue eyes, adorable chubby cheeks, and has her dad and mom wrapped around her little fingers. And who wouldn't want to just eat her up? She's such a cutie! Emerson will celebrate her first birthday in June, so we headed to Goodale Park on Sunday to take some family pictures and commemorate the occasion with a good-ol-fashion cake smash! It was a little chilly on Sunday and Emerson was such a trooper! After a few sad faces we knew it was time for the cake smash! Mommy's DIY streamer background was a Pinterest win and the adorable ombre cake was the perfect finishing touch. Emerson might not have been a big fan of the cake smash, but she sure did look adorable during it! Keep smiling! Kaylina
