Phil + Amy: Muirfield Village Golf Club Wedding | Dublin, Ohio

sunset session / weddings

Phil and Amy's wedding last weekend at Muirfield Village Golf Club in Dublin, Ohio was a beautiful day that Goldilocks herself would be proud of - not too hot, not too cold, just right. Although it rained heavily that morning, the skies were clear by the time these amazing people said "I do" at Saints Simon & Jude church in West Jefferson. The good fortune and perfect conditions continued for the rest of the day as Phil and Amy spent time with their closest friends and family while celebrating the love that brought them all together. A gorgeous wedding for two incredible souls - it's just right. After a beautiful ceremony, Amy, Phil, and their bridal party piled into a limo and headed down to their favorite home-away-from-home: OSU. We took photos in the rotunda of the Horseshoe and then paid a visit to the north-west end of the Oval for some photos in front of University Hall before heading to Muirfield Village for some photos of Amy & Phil by themselves on the links. The reception at Muirfield was incredible - heart felt speeches, amazing food, great cake, and a lively dance floor filled the night. Phil and Amy made time around sunset to sneak outside for a few minutes and it was totally worth it - dancing on the 18th green with the sun setting behind you should be a new wedding event, because these two make it look like so much fun. Amy and Phil - thank you SO much for letting us be a part of your big day! I hope you're having the best time in Aruba and getting in some well-deserved relaxation! Congratulations and best of luck!! Keep smiling! Kaylina
