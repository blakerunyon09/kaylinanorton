Meet the Mohlers: Family Pictures and Senior Portraits

family / portraits / seniors

In my opinion families don't get their pictures taken together nearly often enough. So when Lori, asked if we could add a mini-family session on to Trevor's senior portraits, I was thrilled to say yes. We started out our combo session with about 20 minutes of family pictures at Smeck Farms in Baltimore, Ohio. And let me say, I love this family. It such an honor to take pictures for a family as sweet and caring as this one. After family pictures it was officially Trevor's time to shine. Trevor goes to Liberty Union High School in Baltimore, Ohio. He's on the track team and the percussion section leader for the Liberty Union Marching Lions. Trevor was the perfect model, taking direction not only from me but also from his mom and dad as they had ideas for pictures. To the Mohlers, thank you so much for spending the evening with me. I had so much fun exploring Smeck Farm with you - I always love finding a new location! To Trevor, thanks for putting up with me and my crazy instructions. You're the best and I know that the future has amazing things in store for you. Keep smiling! Kaylina
