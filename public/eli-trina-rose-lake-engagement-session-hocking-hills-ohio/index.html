Eli + Trina: Rose Lake Engagement Session | Hocking Hills, Ohio

engagements

I am so excited to share Trina and Eli's engagement session at Rose Lake in Hocking Hills. These two were so much fun and I promise that every laugh you see is genuine. They may have been nervous for their session (everyone thinks they're awkward in front of the camera), but they crushed it! Trina and Eli decided to start their session at Rose Lake (aka Hocking Hills Reservoir) in the exact spot where Eli originally popped the question. We spent some time exploring the area, fighting with leaves, climbing fallen trees, and took a lot of pictures along the way. Eli has a friend who lives on land in the area with some AMAZING views so we, of course, had to stop by just before sunset to truly appreciate it. Trina and Eli - I cannot wait for your wedding in October! You guys are so much fun and I know your wedding day is going to be incredible! Keep smiling! Kaylina
