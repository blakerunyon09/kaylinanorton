Vendors I Love: Artifact Uprising

for clients / tips

You know how sometimes you come across a new product that you're so excited about you want to literally tell everyone you know? Well, every single one of Artifact Uprising's products is like that for me. A couple of months ago, I came across Artifact Uprising's Instagram and immediately fell in love. I decided to take advantage of a sale they were having on their Square Print Sets and ended up ordering three sets because I couldn't get enough! The best part? They can take the images off of your Instagram account and put them on beautiful, matte paper that will make you feel like your Instagram pictures belong in a gallery. I had about a billion pictures of my cats, Tom and Shnicks, that I had never printed out before because I took them with my phone, but Artifact Uprising's square prints were the perfect solution. Now I have tangible proof I'm a crazy cat lady.   When it came time to order Christmas Cards, I went straight to Artifact Uprising. I absolutely loved the luxurious feel of the paper from the square prints and knew it would be perfect for this year's Christmas cards. While we chose to go a more traditional route, Artifact Uprising does offer these amazing 3-in-1 cards - it's a greeting card, gift tag, and photo print all in one! I absolutely love when I find new companies that are doing exciting things the right way. Artifact Uprising is dedicated to providing amazing quality prints on sustainable, recycled paper and their love and dedication to their craft is evident in every way. Print you pictures, people! (Even if they are just your Instagram pictures) Keep smiling! Kaylina
