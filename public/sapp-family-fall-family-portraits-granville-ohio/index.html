Sapp Family: Fall Family Portraits | Granville, Ohio

family / portraits

I love this little family. Caleb, Alicia, Reagan, and Rory are the sweetest family and so much fun to photograph! Going in to the session, we were a little worried about the weather, but this fall evening ended with the most gorgeous golden light. We met near the library in Granville, Ohio for our session and explored the streets of downtown Granville. Reagan led the way and helped pick out all of our backdrops (and I personally think she did an amazing job). While Reagan did the work, Rory won us all over with his old man faces and slobbery grins. Can we take a moment to appreciate the incredible job Alicia did in coordinating outfits for the whole family? Perfect fall family photo outfits. Nailed it. Keep smiling! Kaylina
