Picking a wedding photographer is kind of a big deal. Unlike your caterer or florist, your wedding photographer will be following you around all day and occasionally telling you what to do. That's why I think that getting along with your photographer is so important. Matching friendship bracelets aren't a requirement, but I do enjoy becoming friends with my brides and grooms!

If you've had the opportunity to click around this website, check out my blog, or briefly stalk me on social media and STILL might want to chat - please shoot me an email! I would love to get to know you and see how I can make your love story, engagement, or wedding a little more fun through pictures.

If you have any burning questions about photography, my services, or what show to start next on Netflix - I welcome those too.
