Meet Hudson: Lifestyle Newborn Photos | Newark, Ohio

baby / portraits

This fall I had the pleasure of shooting maternity pictures for Brooke and Dan. They were expecting their first baby together and could not be more excited for the arrival of their little one. They wanted the gender of the baby to be a surprise, and although they had their own suspicions, it wasn't until this little one arrived did they know for sure… It's a Boy!

Hudson is such an adorable little guy who already has mommy and daddy wrapped around his tiny fingers. In addition to being loved on by mommy and daddy, Hudson has an older brother and sister who are pretty big fans as well. Hudson's newborn session last Sunday was so much fun to shoot and he did such a good job of being a little model.

Welcome to the world, Mr. Hudson - you are so loved!

Keep smiling! Kaylina
