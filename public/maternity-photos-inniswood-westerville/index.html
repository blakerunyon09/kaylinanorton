Maternity Photos: Inniswood Gardens - Westerville, Ohio

baby / family / portraits

This Memorial Day weekend was so much fun and one of the highlights was photographing Nicole and her amazing family. Nicole and Brian are already amazing parents to their adorable daughter, Addie, but soon they'll be pulling double duty when they welcome another little one to the family in a couple months. And I am so excited to meet her!

We met up for Nicole's family maternity session Saturday afternoon at Inniswood Metro Garden in Westerville. Dodging the rain on the forecast, we had to move the session up to noon, which meant we were photographing in the sweltering heat and humidity that is an Ohio summer. Nicole was an absolute champ though. Never breaking a sweat, just being gorgeous the entire time.

Addie was a bundle of energy the entire time and occasionally stole the show, but I mean come on - she's so cute! And so excited to be a big sister. I am so excited to continue to photograph this family as they make the change to family of four in the coming months! Keep smiling! Kaylina
