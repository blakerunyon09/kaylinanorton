Tim + Brielle: Grace Fellowship Church Wedding | Pickerington, Ohio

first look / weddings

Tim and Brielle's wedding last Friday was absolute perfection. A gorgeous, bright, sunny day that had everyone smiling from ear to ear. Not to mention, it was National Donut Day! Tim and Brie have been together for what feels like forever to me. As I mentioned in the blog about their Hilliard engagement pictures, Tim's been a staple at our family events for years now, so I'm so glad to officially welcome him into the family! Brielle, Tim, and the wedding party got ready at Tim's church in Lancaster, the 5th Avenue Church of Christ. While the girls got ready in the basement, the boys did whatever boys do on the morning of a wedding upstairs. Once everyone had slipped into their wedding attire, we headed down the block for Tim and Brielle's first look. These two are so sweet together. I typically stay focused on the groom for the first look, but Brielle's reaction to seeing Tim as her husband for the first time was my favorite! After some portraits of the happy couple, we joined the bridal party for some more pictures before heading to the church for the vows. Both the ceremony and reception were held at Grace Fellowship Church in Pickerington. Brielle did such an amazing job decorating and filling the church with tiny touches that made the day beautiful and more intimate. After Tim and Brielle officially said "I do" it was time to party! Did I mention, there were donuts? Donuts! Brie and Tim exited that night under a tunnel of glow sticks and lived happily ever after =) Keep smiling! Kaylina
