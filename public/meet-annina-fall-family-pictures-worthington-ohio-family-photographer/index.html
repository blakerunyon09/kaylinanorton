Meet Annina: Fall Family Pictures - Worthington, Ohio Family Photographer

baby / family / portraits

This fall has been full of so many fun, amazing family sessions. And last weekend's session with Annina and her mommy and daddy was another great one for the books! Annina and her family actually live in Chicago, but were in town visiting Annina's cousins Emma and Addison, the adorable little girls from yesterday's blog post, so we arranged for a quick family session for them as well! Annina started out shy, but after some hard work from both her parents and myself, but mostly through the help of the fall leaves, we were finally able to get a couple smiles on her face! Success! Thanks so much to Annina and her amazing parents for hanging out with me for the afternoon! All of the hard work pays off when you're left with images of such a beautiful girl's smile! Keep smiling! Kaylina
