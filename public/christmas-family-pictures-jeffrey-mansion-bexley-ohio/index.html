Christmas Family Pictures: Jeffrey Mansion, Bexley, Ohio

family / portraits

With it being warm for so long this year, I feel like the holidays have kind of snuck up on me, but this week has really gotten me into the holiday mood! Last Saturday we saw the first flurries of the year and I got to shoot the most amazing Christmas themed family pictures at Jeffrey Mansion in Bexley, Ohio! Joey and her parents, Lauren and Julian, were so much fun to spend the afternoon with. We hung out on the mansion's grounds near a row of pine trees where we could really channel the spirit of Christmas. And let me tell you, this family should be professional Christmas card models because they are just too cute! Seriously, how cute are Lauren and Joey's matching headbands?! Joey was so amazing and patient for our whole session that I let her take control for the last few pictures and tell us how she wanted to pose. Love these! Happy Thanksgiving! Kaylina
