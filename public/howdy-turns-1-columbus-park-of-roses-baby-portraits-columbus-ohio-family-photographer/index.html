This little heartbreaker's name is Howdy. He just celebrated his very first birthday at the end of September and I was so happy to spend an hour with him on Sunday. I absolutely love this picture of Howdy and his mom. So sweet! Happy Birthday Howdy! Keep smiling! Kaylina
