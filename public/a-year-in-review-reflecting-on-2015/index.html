A Year in Review: Reflecting on 2015 Weddings

personal / weddings

I'm going to be honest, this is one of my favorite posts to write every year. I love looking back at the year and taking one more opportunity to show off the amazing weddings I was blessed to capture and the couple I was lucky enough to get to know. 2015 was an amazing year, we shot 14 weddings and captured even more engagements, senior portraits, families, and brand new babies throughout the year. We shot our first out of state wedding this year and continued to explore Columbus through wedding photography! Not only was 2015 a great year for Kaylina Norton Photography, but it was also a pretty decent year personally - getting married to my best friend and partner in crime might have been the highlight! So here's a look back at the amazing couples who entrusted us with the great task of capturing their memories and love-I can never thank my brides and grooms enough for believing in me and letting me be a part of their big days! Keep smiling! Kaylina
