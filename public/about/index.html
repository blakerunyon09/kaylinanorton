MEET kaylina

I began photographing weddings in 2011 and immediately fell in love with the entire journey.

On a wedding day you’ll find me everywhere, from running around the bridal suite in the morning to displaying my mad lip-syncing skills on the dance floor at the end of the night.

I’m there for it all and I love every moment of it.

ABOUT

01

netflix

If I could medal in anything it would be Netflix binging. I'm always looking for good new shows, but I always have The Office on repeat. Favorite show of all time.

02

animal lover

I am admittedly a level 5 crazy cat lady. I have two cats, Tom Marvolo Riddle and Shnickers. They have their own hashtags, a cat stroller (to go on walks), and a cat tent (to sit outside with me when it's nice). It's okay to judge, I know I'm weird.

03

PODCASTS

I am a podcast junkie. True crime series and political podcasts keep my ears happy throughout the week. I could probably use a few more "light-hearted" pods though, so if you have a suggestion - please share!

04

90s MUSIC

It was the time of girl power, boy bands, and pop princesses. I will never turn down an opportunity to sing along with Alanis, dance with N*sync, or get jiggy with Will Smith.

05

A LITTLE DELUSIONAL

Confession time: I may watch a few too many medical and crime dramas. As a result, I believe I can diagnose any ailment and represent you in court. But if I were you, I'd probably ask for a second opinion.

06

THE MESSIEST EATER

So this is a little embarrassing, but I think I might be the messiest eater known to man. I've spilled gravy on my laptop on 3 separate occasions, accidentally dropped chicken bones in a Crate & Barrel (it's a long story), and red pasta sauce is my mortal enemy.

07

CRYING EASILY

I'm a crier. Chick flicks, sad songs, father-daughter dances, seeing an old man eating dinner by himself - I may tear up a little too easily.

08

SWEET TOOTH

I have a massive sweet tooth, but interestingly enough am not really into desserts. The keys to my heart include gummy worms, Starburst, and sour patch kids.

I grew up in the age of Myspace and top 8s, so I'm always ready to take an online quiz or share too much information about myself.
