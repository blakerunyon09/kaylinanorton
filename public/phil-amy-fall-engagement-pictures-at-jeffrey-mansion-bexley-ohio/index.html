Phil + Amy: Fall Engagement Pictures at Jeffrey Mansion | Bexley, Ohio

engagements

I may have shot Phil and Amy's engagement session 4 months ago, but I can still remember just how fun this engagement session was. Amy & Phil were finishing off their weekend with me after completing the classes required by their church to get married, so the whole weekend was just a series of staring into each other's eyes and saying nice things about the other. We kept joking that by the time they were done with their engagement pictures they'd have to take a couple days off from looking at each other. Phil and Amy met me at Jeffrey Mansion in Bexley for their engagement pictures in October, for a perfect fall engagement session. They were adorable, amazing models who laughed, cuddled, and tickled on cue. I could not have asked for a better session to wrap up right before heading into my vacation to Italy. Amy and Phil, thank you so much for being such an obnoxiously cute couple! You make my job easy and even now I can't stop smiling at these photos - you guys are amazing together! Keep smiling! Kaylina
