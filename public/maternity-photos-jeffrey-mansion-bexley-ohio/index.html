Maternity Photos: Jeffrey Mansion - Bexley, Ohio

baby / couples / portraits

Full disclosure, Dana is one of my favorite humans ever. So when she reached out to ask if I'd be willing to photograph her maternity pictures for her and James, her amazing husband, I said YES! These two by themselves are absolutely adorable and I know that their little one will be just too cute to handle! We shot their maternity session Sunday morning at Jeffrey Mansion in Bexley. And although it was a surprisingly warm day, the trees and landscaping at Jeffrey Mansion still gave us a little taste of fall. I loved taking a few pictures of Dana by herself because she looks amazing!! She has the most adorable baby bump and is hands down gorgeous! We finished James and Dana's session on the back steps of Jeffrey Mansion with some of my favorite shots of the whole morning. Dana and James, thanks so much for spending your morning with me! I loved catching up with you guys and I cannot wait for baby G to make her appearance! Keep smiling! Kaylina
