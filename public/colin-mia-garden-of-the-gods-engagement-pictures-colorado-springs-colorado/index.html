Colin + Mia: Garden of the Gods Engagement Pictures | Colorado Springs, Colorado

engagements

Have you ever been so excited about something, that you just couldn't wait? This is me, with this session. I met Colin and Mia at High Point Overlook at the Garden of the Gods in Colorado Spring, Colorado less than 24 hours ago and here I am blogging their adorable engagement session. I just couldn't wait to share these images! Colin and Mia met in college in Boston and even though neither of them were sure they wanted to spend their college experience "tied down", when you meet the right person - you just know. And when these two are together, there's no doubt that they're meant to be. So they began seeing each other and here they are, almost six years later, with a wedding planned for this July at Mia's parent's vacation home in Plymouth. The weather last night at Garden of the Gods was perfect. And although the parking lot was packed, we were able to find some amazing spots to take pictures, get to know each other, and have some fun. Colin and Mia brought along their incredibly sweet pup, Lorna for some pictures as well. And shout out to their MVF (most valuable friend) Mike who was the best puppy wrangler we could ask for while Lorna was off camera. Colin and Mia - thank you so much for making the drive down to Garden of the Gods for your engagement session! I had so much fun with you two and hope you guys have an absolutely incredible wedding in July! Wishing you the best! Keep smiling! Kaylina
