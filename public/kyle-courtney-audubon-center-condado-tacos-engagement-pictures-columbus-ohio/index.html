Kyle + Courtney: Audubon Center & Condado Tacos Engagement Pictures | Columbus, Ohio

engagements

Can I start by saying that anyone who is interested in incorporating tacos into their engagement session, already has at least 3 gold stars, a thumbs up, and a check plus in my book? When Courtney mentioned that she might want to make a stop at Condado Tacos at the end of her and Kyle's engagement session, I was ready to pretty much make her a best friends forever bracelet. Not only does Courtney have amazing taste in food, she's always got herself a pretty sweet guy. I met up with Kyle and Courtney in the fall at Grange Insurance Audubon Center at the Scioto Audubon Metro Park in downtown Columbus. It was extremely cold out, but these two used it as an excuse to cuddle and be abso-stinking-lutely adorable. Already knowing Courtney a little bit, I was so excited to see that Kyle was perfect for her. When we headed in side for warmth and he willingly took his turn twirling and did a DEAD ON bull-frog impersonation when reading a children's story out loud to us, I knew he was the one for Courtney. After we spent a good amount of time inside the Grange Audubon Center, we headed downtown for some food and drinks at Condado Tacos. I loved sitting and chatting with them as we waited for the food and of course, snapped some photos along the way. These two were so much fun to photograph and I cannot wait for their big day in October! Keep smiling! Kaylina
